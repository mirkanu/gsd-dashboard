## Decisions Made

- **Cloudflare → ngrok pivot:** User had no Cloudflare account. Cloudflare named tunnels require account + domain ownership. ngrok free tier gives a static domain with just email signup.
- **ngrok authtoken approach:** Simpler than cloudflared login (no browser OAuth needed — just paste token)
- **Static domain:** `heathless-art-unharsh.ngrok-free.dev` — permanent, never rotates, one-time Railway config
